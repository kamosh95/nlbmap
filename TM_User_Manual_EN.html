Seller Details & Map - User Manual

01 User Registration (Sign Up)

If you do not have an account, follow these steps to register a new user account.
Step 1: User Registration Form
  • URL: Open your browser and go to https://sys.nlb.lk/map/signup.php.
  • System Role: Select "Field Agent (TM)" if you are a Territory Manager.
  • Personal Details: Enter your full name, Employee Number, mobile number, and official email.
  • Username & Password: Choose a login username and a strong password.
  • Submit: Click "Submit Registration" to finish.
IMPORTANT: After registration, your account must be approved by the System Administrator (Admin) before you can log in.

02 System Login

Once your account is approved, log in to the system using your credentials.
Step 2: Secure Login Page
  • URL: Navigate to https://sys.nlb.lk/map/login.php.
  • Credentials: Enter your registered Username and Password.
  • Sign In: Click the blue button to enter the portal.

03 Main Menu (TM Dashboard)

The main dashboard provides quick access to data entry and search tools.
Step 3: Territory Manager Dashboard Overview

04 Registering a New Seller (New Seller Entry)

How to enter new counter seller details into the system.
Step 4: Data Entry Form with GPS and Photos
  • Dealer & Agent Codes: Select the corresponding dealer/agent.
  • Location Capture: Paste a Google Maps Link or use the "Get GPS" button.
  • Images: Upload the seller's photo and counter views (Rear view is optional).
  • Submit: Review all details and click "Submit" to save.

05 Search & Export Data (View Details)

View the list of registered sellers and download reports.
Step 5: Filtering and Exporting the Seller List
  • Use the search bar to find sellers by name or code.
  • Filter data by Province or District regions.
  • Excel Export: Click the "Export" button to download a spreadsheet report.
